REDE PECAS - WHATSAPP CONVERSATION SCRIPT
Example used: customer "Vivek", vehicle Honda Accord 2003
(User = what the customer types/taps | System = what the bot replies)

================================================================
1. FIRST CONTACT & REGISTRATION
================================================================

User: Hi

System: Welcome to Rede Pecas!

We're Angola's auto parts marketplace - we find the right parts
for your vehicle as fast as possible.

To help you better, let's set up your profile quickly.

What's your name?

User: Vivek Makwana

System: Nice to meet you, Vivek!

Do you have a NIF (tax ID) to include on invoices?
(useful if you're buying on behalf of a company)
(buttons: Yes, I have a NIF / No, thanks)

User: No, thanks

System: What's your preferred delivery address?

Example: Bairro Morro Bento, Rua da Samba, No 12, Luanda

(reply "skip" if you'd rather provide it when placing an order)

User: Bairro Morro Bento, Rua da Samba, No 45, Luanda

System: Profile created successfully, Vivek!

Next time you message us, I'll already recognize you.

Now I need to identify your vehicle. Pick an option:
(buttons: I have the VIN / Send a photo / Manual entry)

--- if "Yes, I have a NIF": ---
System: Great! Type your NIF number.
User: [NIF number]
(then goes straight to the address question above)

--- if the customer goes quiet mid-registration and returns later: ---
System: Let's continue your registration!
System: [repeats the exact question for the step they stalled on]


================================================================
2. VEHICLE IDENTIFICATION (3 possible paths)
================================================================

--- PATH A: VIN ---

User: I have the VIN

System: Perfect! Send the chassis number (VIN) - 17 characters,
found on the vehicle document or stamped on the chassis itself.

User: 1HGCM82633A004352

System: Identifying the vehicle from the chassis number...

System: Vehicle identified!

Honda Accord 2003 - 2.3L - Gasoline - Sedan

Is this your car?
(buttons: Yes, that's it / No, different car)

--- if the VIN isn't recognized by NHTSA: ---
System: I couldn't identify that chassis number.

Let's fill in the details manually. What's the make of the
vehicle?

Example: Toyota, Mercedes, Volvo...
[continues into PATH C - Manual entry, below]


--- PATH B: DOCUMENT PHOTO ---

User: Send a photo

System: Perfect! Take a clear photo of the vehicle document
(registration/title) and send it here.

User: [sends photo of the registration document]

System: Got the photo. Reading the document's data...

System: Data read from the document!

Honda Accord 2003 - 2.3L - Gasoline - Plate: LD-45-67-AB

Is this your car?
(buttons: Yes, that's it / No, different car)

--- possible errors at this step: ---
- Couldn't download the image:
  "I couldn't download the image. Please try sending it again,
  or reply 'I don't have it' to fill in the details manually."
- Error processing the document:
  "Something went wrong processing the document. Please try
  again, or reply 'I don't have it' to fill in the details
  manually."
- Image isn't a vehicle document:
  "That image doesn't look like a vehicle document
  (registration/title). You can send the chassis number (VIN)
  as text, try another photo, or reply 'I don't have it'..."
- Photo unreadable:
  "[reason]. Please try again with a clearer photo, making sure
  that: the image is well lit and in focus, the document is
  fully visible, the text is legible with no glare or shadows."
- Essential data missing (make/model):
  "I read the document but essential data is missing
  (make/model). Please try another photo, or reply
  'I don't have it'..."


--- PATH C: MANUAL ENTRY ---

User: Manual entry

System: No problem! Let's fill in the details manually.

What's the make of the vehicle?

Example: Toyota, Mercedes, Volvo...

User: Honda

System: Honda

Now tell me the model of the vehicle.

Example: Hilux, L200, Actros, Sprinter, Ranger...

User: Accord

System: Honda Accord

What year is the vehicle?

Example: 2015, 2018, 2020...

User: 2003

System: Honda Accord 2003

What's the engine number? (optional)

This number matters for engine parts, servicing, and
maintenance.

If you don't know it, reply "don't know" and we'll continue.

User: don't know

System: Great! I've saved your vehicle's details:

Honda Accord 2003

Now tell me which part you need and I'll search our stock.

--- invalid year (not 4 digits, or out of range): ---
System: Invalid year. Please enter the year with 4 digits.
Example: 2018


--- CONFIRMATION (after VIN or Photo) ---

--- if "No, different car": ---
System: No problem! Let's fill in the details manually.
What's the make of the vehicle?
Example: Toyota, Mercedes, Volvo...
[enters PATH C - Manual entry, above. Only the rejected attempt
is deleted, never any other vehicle the customer already has
confirmed]
